IOT-Based Smart Parking Management System

A smart parking solution that helps users find available parking slots using IoT sensors and real-time updates.

  • Problem: Difficulty in finding parking spaces.
  • Solution: Ultrasonic sensors detect vehicle presence.
  • Working: If distance is less than 10 cm, slot is occupied.
  • Benefits: Saves time, fuel, and reduces traffic congestion.
