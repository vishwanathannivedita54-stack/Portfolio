1.IOT-Based Smart Parking Management System

  • Problem:Parking spaces are hard to find due to increasing vehicles, limited parking areas, traffic congestion, and time wasted searching for slots.
  • Solution:Uses ultrasonic sensors to detect vehicle presence, LEDs to show slot status, and a mobile app for real-time parking updates.
  • Technologies
    1. ESP8266 NodeMCU
    2. HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor
    3. Arduino IDE(Embedded C)
    4. Blynk IOT platform
  • Working:If the measured distance is less than 10 cm, the slot is occupied; otherwise, it is available. Status is shown through LEDs and the mobile app.
  • Benefits: Saves time and fuel, reduces traffic congestion, low-cost, easy to install, and scalable for smart city applications.
  • Future Enhancements:Online slot booking, automatic gate control, payment integration, notifications, and AI-based vehicle counting.
  • Improves parking management, reduces human effort, and supports smart city development.
